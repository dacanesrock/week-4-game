Treasure Hunting for dummies

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score to a randomly generated number. You lose if your score total exceeds that random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of the crystals will change.
